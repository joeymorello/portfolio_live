Walmart Museum Videobooth

ROLE:
Lead Software Developer

TOOLS:
TouchDesigner, Python, Whisper.ai, Mediapipe, AWS


Bentonville, Arkansas

2025

Working for Bridgewater Studio, I lead the development of this interactive videobooth for The Walmart Museum. The goal of the project was to give museum guests a chance to speak on what they believe the future of retail will be. The videobooth records up to 30 seconds of the guest speaking. Once the recording is saved, the file is uploaded to AWS where the video is shared to the guest via a QR code to a microsite.
