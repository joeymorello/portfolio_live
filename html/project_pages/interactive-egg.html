Interactive Egg

ROLE:
Lead Software Developer

TOOLS:
TouchDesigner, Arduino


2024

This is a prototype for an interactive projection mapped egg sculpture. I was tasked with building the core functionality and exploring how content can be created and presented on the sculpture. I handled projection mapping the egg and creating IR based touch point blob tracking with touch point haptic feedback for interactions on the surface of the egg. I also created a TOF sensor array for room scale people tracking around the sculpture.
